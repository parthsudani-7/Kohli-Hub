ODI Records

Virat Kohli holds the record for the fastest century in ODIs and has scored over 12,000 runs with an average above 58.

Test Cricket

Kohli has 27 Test centuries and is known for his consistency and leadership in the longest format.

T20 World Cup

Kohli starred in the T20 World Cup with match-winning performances, including a memorable 82*.

Captaincy

Under his leadership, India became the No. 1 Test team in 2016. He has won multiple series and is praised for his aggressive captaincy style.

Individual Awards

Kohli has won the ICC ODI Player of the Year multiple times (2012, 2017, 2018). He is also the recipient of the Arjuna Award and Padma Shri.

Brand and Influence

Beyond cricket, Kohli is a global icon with millions of followers on social media. He has endorsements with major brands and advocates for fitness and mental health.
